About



In an era where organizations are overwhelmed by black-box AI "solutions" promising to derive insights from masses of linguistic data, I provide the human expertise necessary to deliver both strategic intelligence and precise technical translations grounded in real-world technical and cultural context.

I operate on the principle that language is a reflection of human intention, not just a series of data points. As such, deep understanding requires a synthesis of both linguistic expertise and technical rigor to capture what is actually being communicated. The result is bringing to light the vital details that automated tools and generalist services miss.

On the analytics side, I use my background in scientific research and Japanese, Mandarin Chinese, and Russian to help clients sort through masses of linguistic data to answer questions they care about. I build custom analyses utilizing tried-and-tested natural language processing methodologies, and I ensure that findings are not only empirically backed but are also contextually grounded within their respective industries and regions.

On the translation side, I provide high-precision human translation with speciality in technical documentation and patent filings. By treating translation as a rigorous investigative process rather than simple word-by-word replacement, I ensure that the final product maintains the integrity of the original technical specifications and legal nuances.

At the core of my work is a commitment to transparency and security. I don't rely on third-party AI platforms that risk your data but instead I develop independent solutions tailored to the specific technical and linguistic requirements of your project.

I look forward to working with you.

- Devin

Analytics

In an era of information overload, there is plenty of information to go around, but it becomes increasingly challenging to make any sense of it. I develop custom solutions to collect and transform raw multilingual data into clear, empirical insights that can help you answer questions that are important to you.

Analytics Process
I carry out my analytics projects according to four steps.

Step 1: Requirements & Scope
First is a consultation to identify your key questions. For example, you may want to track technical terminology in Japanese patent filings, analyze sentiments across Russian social media, identify emerging narratives in Chinese industry reports, etc. Whatever the case, we work together to define the parameters, and I provide a project-specific quote.

Step 2: Data Collection & Cleansing
I build custom scripts and automation tools specifically for your project in order to capture the data needed. Alternatively, if your question requires human responses, I can arrange online surveys or experiments to capture the necessary data. I then clean this data so that it is ready to be analyzed to answer your questions.

Step 3: Analysis
Using both computational tools and linguistic expertise, I analyze the gathered data. I interpret both the linguistic and technical context behind the patterns to identify key takeaways.

Step 4: Insights
I deliver a comprehensive report that provides empirically backed answers to your original questions. These reports turn the complex multilingual patterns from Step 3 into a strategy suited to your needs.

Technical Translation

Though many get by with Google Translate and other tools, machine translation tends to break down when the subject matter requires deep understanding of real-world systems, rules, cultural contexts, etc. It is precisely in these scenarios that the stakes are too high for machine errors, and the knowledge, precision, and consistency of an expert human translator is required. Drawing from my technical research background and professional experience, I offer technical and scientific translations, specializing in patents.

Translation Process
I carry out my translations according to four steps.

Step 1: Initial Reading & Quote
Please send me an email at devin@sen-linguistics.com with a scan and/or description of the document(s). I will lightly review the documents to determine the amount of time needed and the cost. I will send you a quote with these details and ask you to confirm it before I begin work.

Step 2: Translation
After you accept the quote, I will first spend time analyzing the style, terminology, and subject matter. I may ask you for further clarification on certain details. This analysis is important for ensuring accuracy and naturalness in the final translation. Using this, I then produce a rough draft of the translation.

Step 3: Editing
I perform several rounds of checks and send you a sample of the completed document(s). If you have any required changes, I will make revisions as necessary.

Step 4: Final Delivery
After agreeing on a final version, I will deliver it to you in whichever format you require. I will also attach an invoice and request payment within 30 days.

Analytics

Q: What kind of linguistic data do you analyze?
A: As a very simple rule, so long as it's digital text and I can access it, it's fair game. With that said, my linguistic background allows me to tackle more niche cases in languages other than English (i.e., Japanese, Chinese, Russian). While any computational linguist might be able to make processing tools for such languages, I am able to do the extra work of verifying whether the results of such tools really make sense or not. In addition, if the data required is not on the internet, I can also arrange online surveys and experiments to obtain data from human respondents.

Q: Why use custom scripts instead of off-the-shelf AI tools?
A: Custom scripts provide full transparency and data privacy. Unlike black-box AI models, tools built in Python or R allow for granular control over how your data is processed, ensuring that the final insights are empirically sound and not subject to the hallucinations or privacy risks associated with public AI platforms.

Q: Can you work with my existing internal data?
A: Yes. If you have a large corpus of multilingual documents or database exports, I can build custom tools to clean, categorize, and analyze that data.

Translation

Q: Do you provide certified translations?
A: In the US, there is no recognized legal process for certification. However, upon request, I am happy to attach a statement of responsibility and accuracy with my signature.

Q: I already have a draft in English. Can you edit it for me?
A: If you already have content in English but want to make sure that it is accurate and natural, I am happy to provide editing services.

Q: Do you also translate from English into Japanese and Mandarin?
A: To ensure accuracy and naturalness, I only translate into my native English.

Q: How is my data stored?
A: Whatever documents you send to me will be stored offline and under password protection. For any software used, I turn off any services that connect your data to third parties.
