What is Sen Linguistics?

Though AI has come a long way and can now write and translate seemingly convincingly, it has a fatal weakness in that it can only jumble together the patterns it has seen. This makes it useful for highly repetitive and standard content with clear context, but human language (and life) is filled with creativity and often critically depends on many pieces of cultural information which AI has never seen and can't comprehend.

Sen Linguistics fills the human, real-world gaps where AI can't deliver. I help people throughout the world transform complex information into clear English so that important messages can be shared across borders and cultures. To accomplish this, I offer two services:

  • Translation for bringing highly context-dependent information from Japanese and Chinese into clear, accurate English
  • Editing for helping you develop a straightforward, persuasive voice in English writing that doesn't sound like a machine

Why Me?

I'm a scientist

Not all translators come with a first-hand experience working in science and academia. My experience in scientific research has not only given me insight into the workings of a wide range of technical systems but has also made me unafraid of diving into deep research to get the details right.

I have in-house experience

I've completed rigorous training in translation at the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO), gaining an understanding of technical language in a wide range of fields and an eye for handling high-stakes content.

I have principles, not just pattern matching

AI, by its design, has no idea what constitutes 'good' and 'bad' – only what is probable. This is particularly dangerous because highly probable translations are often flat out wrong and meaningfully change the nature of an invention, an argument, or even history. I operate on principles beyond probability that ensure a result that is actually reliable.
