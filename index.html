About



In an era overwhelmed by one-size-fits-all AI products that promise instant insights and top-quality translations, true value lies in human expertise that can navigate linguistic data with principle and purpose. I operate on the idea that language is a highly complex product of human intention, not just a series of data points. As such, deep understanding requires a synthesis of both linguistic expertise and scientific rigor to capture what is actually being communicated and bring to light the vital details that automated tools and generalist data science services miss. If you are wondering whether incorporating linguistic data into your project is the right move or looking for an expertly crafted translation, I am happy to help.

Strategic Linguistic Data Consulting
I help you determine if and how linguistic data (from web-scraped corpora to human surveys) can serve your project goals. Where necessary, I build custom prototypes and analytical reports that help you know what to expect before you commit to a particular path.

High-Precision Translation
I deliver expertly crafted translations for high-stakes documentation in technology and science, specializing in patents. I translate from Japanese, Chinese, and Russian into English, and I treat translation as an investigative process, not a simple word-replacement task, ensuring that the technical accuracy and legal nuances of the original text remain intact.

Security Without Compromise
At the core of my work is a commitment to privacy and security. I don't rely on third-party AI platforms that risk your data but instead I tailor my work to the specific technical and linguistic requirements of your project.

Linguistic Data Consulting

Admist information overload, there is plenty of linguistic data to go around, but it becomes increasingly challenging to make any sense of it. I help you determine whether incorporating linguistic data into your project is the right move, and if so, how to incorporate it best.

Consulting Process
I carry out projects according to four steps.

Step 1: Defining the Problem
First is identifying the problem you want to solve. For example, do you have particular questions you want to answer? D you want to improve an existing process? Whatever the case, we work together to define the problem precisely.

Step 2: Investigation
Some projects are a natural fit for using linguistic data, while others are less so. At this step, I conduct research on your problem and identify solutions which employ linguistic data. I identify both the potential benefits and costs of gathering and employing linguistic data.

Step 3: Report and/or Prototype
I deliver a report detailing the results of my research, explaining how linguistic data may or may not be useful to your project. In addition, a small prototype may be included depending on the project.

Step 4: Takeaways
We discuss the report and my recommendations for your project. I answer any questions you may have about how to proceed.

Technical Translation

Though many get by with Google Translate and other tools, machine translation tends to break down when the subject matter requires deep understanding of real-world systems, rules, cultural contexts, etc. It is precisely in these scenarios that the stakes are too high for machine errors, and the knowledge, precision, and consistency of an expert human translator is required. Drawing from my technical research background and professional experience, I offer technical and scientific translations, specializing in patents.

Translation Process
I carry out my translations according to four steps.

Step 1: Initial Reading & Quote
Send me a scan and/or description of the document(s). I will lightly review the documents to determine the amount of time needed and the cost. I will send you a quote with these details and ask you to confirm it before I begin work.

Step 2: Translation
After you accept the quote, I will first spend time analyzing the style, terminology, and subject matter. I may ask you for further clarification on certain details. This analysis is important for ensuring accuracy and naturalness in the final translation. Using this, I then produce a rough draft of the translation.

Step 3: Editing
I perform several rounds of checks and send you a sample of the completed document(s). If you have any required changes, I will make revisions as necessary.

Step 4: Final Delivery
After agreeing on a final version, I will deliver it to you in whichever format you require. I will also attach an invoice and request payment within 30 days.

Linguistic Data Consulting

Q: What kind of linguistic data do you work with?
A: As a very simple rule, so long as it is digital text and I can access it, I can work with it. With that said, my linguistic background allows me to tackle more niche cases in languages other than English (i.e., Japanese, Chinese, Russian). While a generalist data scientist might be able to make processing tools for such languages, I am able to do the work of verifying whether using such tools would really make sense or not in the context of your project.

Q: Can you work with my existing internal data?
A: Yes. If you have a large corpus of multilingual documents or database exports, I am happy to work with that.

A: If you already have content in English but want to make sure that it is accurate and natural, I am happy to provide editing services.

Q: Do you also translate from English into other languages?
