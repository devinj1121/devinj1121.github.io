What is Sen Linguistics?

Innovative ideas are powerful, but they are often too difficult for even the latest AI tools to handle without making mistakes. I help people throughout the world transform complex technical information into clear English so that exciting ideas can be shared across borders. To accomplish this, I offer two services:

  • Technical Editing for ensuring technical English writings and presentations are clear and persuasive without sounding like bland AI
  • Technical Translation for bringing technical information from Japanese and Chinese into English without losing crucial details

Why Me?

I'm a scientist myself

Not all translators come with a first-hand experience working in science and technology. My experience in scientific research has given me insight into the workings of a wide range of technical systems, and I'm not afraid of intricate details.

I have in-house experience

I've completed rigorous training in translation at the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO), gaining an understanding of patent language in a wide range of fields and an eye for sensitive legal wording.

I have principles, not just pattern matching (Unlike AI)

AI, by its design, has no idea what constitutes 'good' and 'bad' – only what is probable. This is particularly dangerous because highly probable translations are often flat out wrong and change the nature of an invention, a legal argument, etc. I operate on principles beyond probability that ensure a result that is actually reliable.
